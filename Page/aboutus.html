About


PGKNT is an informational Digital Safety Enhancement channel on the video site platform YouTube. PGKNT is a channel that enlightens internet viewers about topics related to their safety in the cyber world, by posting original researches, making tech tutorials and doing app reviews on applications that are good for privacy, security, and finance. PGKNT Digital Safety Channel is a Pro-Privacy channel and it supports other similar channels as well. It also allows other people to let their opinions shine by allowing people to share researches or suggest ideas for a content and letting their voice out.
