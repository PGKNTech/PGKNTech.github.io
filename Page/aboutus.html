About


PGKNT is a Philipppine-based YouTube channel which gives simplified information about Digital Safety. I believe that people need these information to be aware of things that can help them protect themselves from various cyber threats that can affect an individual's daily life. This is made for people who wants to know about things related to digital safety that is factual, and easy to understand.

Channel Updates: Improvements have been made to the channel and starting from May 28, PGKNT will release videos that will give scenarios to better help audiences to understand what we are telling to them. Use of presentations will be used for visual aid.
