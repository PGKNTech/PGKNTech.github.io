PGKNT Digital Safety Channel on YouTube


PGKNT is an informational and kind of educational channel on the video site platform YouTube. PGKNT is composed of a one man show giving information about topics related to digital safety. It also makes videos on tech tutorials, and app reviews. PGKNT is a Pro-Privacy supporter and advocate, and is dedicated to letting people know about things related to their online safety safety that they are not fully aware of. It aims in making information as simple so that more will be more enlightened.


"It does not matter to me how many supporters I got, how many views I got, or how many likes and shares. All I, PGKNT cares about is that people learn through my videos, and that they become an advocate for enhancing the safety and security of all of us on the internet and within technology."

-PGKNT
