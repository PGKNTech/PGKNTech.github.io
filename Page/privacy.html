Policy

Personal Data


PGKNT swears to not collect or ask for Personal Data without consent unless required or needed to. Any website owned by us will not ask any type of information, and if information is somehow put, it does not collect it. A particular feature in our landing page is the prompt() that asks for your name. Your input is not collected nor stored by PGKNT or by third party services. We also limit Personal Data to Emails, and Facebook and Discord accounts when communicating to us through those services.


Use of Analytics


PGKNT may use a Third Party but Privacy Friendly Analytics service to further improve and maintain the websites we own, and to monitor the usage of those websites. Nevertheless the analytics will not collect personal data, and will not track or monitor your browsing habits. All data from the Analytics services will be Anonymous Usage Data only.


Cookies and other Site Tools


PGKNT will not use any type of cookies or tools like ads and trackers on all of our owned websites that will violate your privacy, we only use tools that protect and respect your privacy. When we use third party services, we may not ensure what tools are used in those services as we do not own them. Our YouTube channel will not include ads but use alternative ways to gain some profit out of making Digital Safety videos.

Use of third party services


PGKNT uses some third party services to communicate with you if you have concerns or would like to have suggestions based on our Youtube Channel or Videos. These services include Facebook, Discord, and Gmail and Forms So I advise you to read their Privacy Policy also to prevent misunderstandings when using those services to communicate with us or something else.

Children's Privacy


PGKNT swears to protect the privacy of Children under 13 years old in compliance with COPPA’s policy and Youtube community Guidelines. We will automatically verify the ages of children firstly before collecting non-identifiable/(may be) personal data through surveys and forms from third party services we use. We also remind that our Videos are not for children as the topics may be not suitable yet for their ages.


Security of your Data


PGKNT ensures that your Data is 100% secure on all electronic storage or on third party storage (if the data is input on a third party we use). We PGKNT Digital Safety will take all steps reasonably necessary to ensure that Your data is treated securely and in accordance with this Privacy Policy and no transfer of Your Personal Data will take place to an organization or a country with or without your consent. Your Data shall stay safe with us.
